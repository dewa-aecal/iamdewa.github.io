About Me | 自己紹介

Hello I am Dewa Ardiana (出羽 アルディアナ), a doctoral student at Ritsumeikan University 立命館大学, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction and Affective Engineering 感性工学. My research focuses on emotion recognition in educational settings, exploring how technology can better understand and support learners' emotional experiences. I enjoy reading books about psychology, culture, and history, which broaden my perspective and inspire my work.
